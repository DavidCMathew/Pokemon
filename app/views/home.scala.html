What is a Pokémon?

The name Pokémon is the romanized contraction of the Japanese brand Pocket Monsters. The term Pokémon collectively refers to the numerous fictional species that have made appearances in Pokémon media

What are the characteristics of a Pokémon?

Every Pokémon is defined by a unique name. Each Pokémon can be of a type such as Fire, Water, Grass etc. and some may have 2 types. Each type has its own weaknesses and strengths, this helps in deciding which Pokémon could be stronger than the other. Each pokemon also has an ability which can give it an edge. Some Pokémon can evolve into stronger forms

Who is a Trainer?

A trainer is one who befriends Pokémon by capturing them. A trainer can have a team of upto 6 Pokémon.
